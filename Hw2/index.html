“Long Live The Web”

09/24/2023

"Berners-Lee discusses the future of the internet and the threats it faces. He emphasizes the importance of an open internet and warns that controlled and closed networks could hinder the development and innovation of the internet. Berners-Lee offers some suggestions on how to protect and strengthen the internet to ensure it continues to promote the free flow of information, communication, and collaboration."
